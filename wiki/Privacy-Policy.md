# Privacy Policy

Yabbyville is a private community for friends. Here's what we collect and why.

## What we collect

- **Your email address** — used to log you in. 
- **Your username and profile info** — whatever you choose to set (display name, avatar, bio). This is visible to other members.
- **Your activity** — messages, reactions, stickers, lists, and anything else you post. This is stored in Firestore and visible to other members.
- **Anonymous page view stats** — via [Umami](https://umami.is), a privacy-friendly analytics tool. No personal data, no cookies, no fingerprinting. We collect this to try and understand how you use the site, and how to make it better both for users as well as more efficient. 

## What we don't collect

- No tracking cookies
- No advertising data
- No third-party analytics that identify you
- No selling of data to anyone

## Who can see your data

Other members of Yabbyville can see your username, profile, and anything you post. Nobody outside the community can access the site — it requires a login.

## How your data is stored

User data is stored in Firebase (Google's cloud infrastructure). Firebase is subject to Google's security and data handling practices.
Analytics are stored locally, on the Yabbyville server. 

## Deleting your data

Want your account or posts removed? Reach out to an admin and we'll sort it.

## Changes

If anything material changes, we'll let you.
